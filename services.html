Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)
Ordering and Shipping


When will my order ship?
On the exact date you ordered and the payment will only be Cash on Delivery.

Can I place an order to be shipped outside of The Philippines?
Due to the perishability of our product, we do not ship outside The Philippines. To shop at one of our International locations.

Can you ship chocolate during warm weather or to warmer parts of the country?
Yes, we absolutely can ship the product during warm weather and to warm climates in The Philippines. all year long. Our shipping system monitors the temperatures where your package is being delivered and selects the appropriate packing material such as cooler boxes and frozen gel packs to ensure that your gift arrives in perfect condition and at no additional cost to you.

Can I ship to more than one person on one order?
Yes, you can ship to multiple different addresses in a single order.
